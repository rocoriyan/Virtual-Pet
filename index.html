Welcome to the Cyberpet Adventure!

In a digital realm filled with pixels and playful chirps, meet Frodo the frog and Daphne the duck. Their cyber adventure begins in a vibrant land of code, where algorithms ripple like water and joy flows through lines of binary.

Frodo, with his virtual leaps,and Daphne, the pixelated explorer, form an extraordinary friendship. As lines of code interwine, their cyber-meadow becomes the backdrop for a story where happiness is measured in bits and playfulness unfolds in every click.

Get ready to feed, play and pet your way through the binary meadow. Can you keep Frodo and Daphne happy or will the code of their caramaderie face a virtual glitch? The journey starts now, where every tap and interaction shapes the cyber-tale of two inseparable companions.
